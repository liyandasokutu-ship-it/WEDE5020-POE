Our Programs and Services

Explore the full range of programmes we offer to empower youth through education, mentorship, and skills development.

At Youth Empowerment Hub, we believe that every young person has the potential to achieve great things when given the right support. All programmes are free of charge and open to qualifying participants.

1. Academic Tutoring

Our academic tutoring programme provides one-on-one and group tutoring sessions for high school learners and university students in subjects including:

  • Mathematics and Physical Science
  • English and Afrikaans
  • Accounting and Business Studies
  • Life Sciences and Geography
  • Computer Applications Technology

Who can apply: High school learners Grade 8 to 12 and first year university students.

Schedule: Monday to Friday 15:00 to 17:00 and Saturdays 09:00 to 12:00.

Enquire About Tutoring

2. Mentorship Programme

Our mentorship programme connects young individuals with experienced professionals who provide guidance in areas such as:

  • Career planning and job searching
  • Goal setting and personal development
  • Leadership and communication skills
  • Entrepreneurship and business guidance
  • Higher education applications and bursaries

Who can apply: University students and unemployed youth aged 18 to 35.

Duration: 6 month mentorship cycles with monthly one on one sessions.

Apply for Mentorship

3. Skills Workshops

Our skills workshops are practical hands on sessions that include:

  • CV writing and LinkedIn profile optimisation
  • Interview preparation and presentation skills
  • Digital literacy and Microsoft Office basics
  • Financial literacy and budgeting
  • Entrepreneurship and small business start up

Who can apply: All youth aged 16 to 35.

Schedule: Monthly Saturday workshops 09:00 to 13:00.

Register for a Workshop

4. Career Guidance

Our career guidance programme offers professional counselling sessions including:

  • Career interest and aptitude assessments
  • Information on tertiary study options and bursaries
  • Job placement assistance and referrals
  • SETA learnerships and skills programmes
  • Networking opportunities with employers

Who can apply: Unemployed youth and school leavers aged 17 to 35.

Schedule: By appointment Tuesdays and Thursdays 10:00 to 15:00.
