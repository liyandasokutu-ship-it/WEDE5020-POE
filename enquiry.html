Ways to Get Involved

As a Participant

If you are a high school learner, university student, unemployed youth, or community member, you can apply to join any of our free programmes.

As a Volunteer

We are always looking for passionate individuals to join our team as volunteer tutors, mentors, or event assistants.

  • Minimum commitment: 2 hours per week
  • All volunteers receive a certificate of participation
  • Training and support provided

As a Sponsor or Donor

Your financial support helps us reach more youth and expand our programmes.

  • Individual donations welcome from any amount
  • Corporate sponsorship packages available
  • Section 18A tax certificates issued upon request
