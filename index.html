Who We Are

Youth Empowerment Hub is a non-profit organisation established in 2022 by a group of university students. We recognised that many young people in local communities lack access to academic support, mentorship, and career guidance.

Our mission is to empower young people through education, mentorship, and skills development — because every young individual deserves an equal opportunity to succeed.

Our Programs

We offer a range of programmes designed to uplift and equip youth with the tools they need.

Academic Tutoring

One-on-one and group tutoring sessions for high school learners and university students across all major subjects.

Mentorship Programme

Connect with experienced mentors who guide you in career planning, personal development, and goal setting.

Skills Workshops

Practical workshops covering CV writing, interview skills, entrepreneurship, and digital literacy.

Career Guidance

Professional career counselling sessions to help unemployed youth find direction and opportunities in the job market.
