About Us

Learn about our story, our mission, and the people behind Youth Empowerment Hub.

Our Story

Youth Empowerment Hub was established in 2022 by a group of passionate university students who saw a pressing need in their communities. They recognised that many young people lacked access to academic support, mentorship, and career guidance.

What began as a small tutoring initiative has grown into a fully-fledged non-profit organisation offering a wide range of programmes designed to uplift and empower the youth of South Africa.

Since our founding, we have reached hundreds of high school learners, university students, and unemployed youth across Gauteng.

Our Mission

To empower young people through education, mentorship, and skills development — ensuring that every youth has the tools and support needed to create a better future for themselves and their communities.

Our Vision

To build a future where all young individuals have equal opportunities to succeed and reach their full potential, regardless of their socioeconomic background or circumstances.

Our Core Values

  • Empowerment: We believe in building the confidence and capabilities of every young person we serve.
  • Inclusivity: We welcome youth from all backgrounds, cultures, and walks of life.
  • Integrity: We operate with honesty, transparency, and accountability in everything we do.
  • Community: We believe that strong communities are built when individuals support one another.
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest quality in all our programmes and services.

Who We Serve

Our programmes are designed to support a diverse range of young individuals, including:

High School Learners

We offer academic tutoring and study skills workshops to help matric learners achieve their best results.

University Students

We provide mentorship and career guidance to help university students navigate their academic journey and future careers.

Unemployed Youth

We run skills development programmes and job readiness workshops to help unemployed youth enter the workforce.

Community Members

We welcome any community member seeking personal growth, new skills, or a supportive network of like-minded individuals.
